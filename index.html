PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Saniya Wire and Machine Co.

specializes in the production of galvanizing plant, electro galvanizing plant, wire drawing machines, and wire machinery. Here is an overview of our product offerings:

Hot Dip Galvanizing plant:

Our galvanizing plant are designed to provide an efficient and reliable process for coating metals with a protective layer of zinc. These machines are used in various industries such as construction cable armor, automotive, and electronics to enhance the corrosion resistance of metal components.

Electro galvanizing plant:

Our electro galvanizing plant utilize an electroplating process to apply a uniform and controlled zinc coating onto metal substrates. These machines are commonly used in the production of wire, sheet metal, and small metal components for industries including electrical, hot dip galvanizing, and hardware.

Wire drawing machinery:

Our wire machinery includes a comprehensive range of equipment and tools to support the wire manufacturing process. This includes wire drawing machines, wire straightening and cutting machines, wire annealing machines, and wire bending machines. These machines are crucial for shaping and processing wires to meet specific dimensional and quality requirements

SPOOL

In the context of wire and cable, a spool is typically used to store or transport lengths of wire and cable. These spools may have flanges or rims that help to keep the wire or cable in place and prevent it from unwinding during transport. Spools may also have a hole in the center to allow for a spindle or other mechanism to be used for unwinding and winding the material.
